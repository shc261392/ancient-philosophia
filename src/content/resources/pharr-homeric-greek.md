*Homeric Greek: A Book for Beginners* by Clyde Pharr (revised 4th edition by John Wright) teaches Homeric Greek by working through the Iliad line by line from the very first lesson. Each chapter introduces new grammar alongside a running vocabulary. Ideal for students who want to read Homer without first mastering Attic Greek.
