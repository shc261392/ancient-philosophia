Dickinson College Commentaries (DCC) offers free, peer-reviewed commentaries on core Greek and Latin texts with vocabulary glosses, grammatical notes, and historical context. Texts include Caesar, Cicero, Ovid, Vergil, and Nepos in Latin, and selections from Thucydides in Greek. The site also provides core vocabulary lists and other learning aids.
