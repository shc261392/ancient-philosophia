*Wheelock's Latin* by Frederic M. Wheelock (7th edition, revised by Richard LaFleur) has been the standard introductory Latin textbook since 1956. It covers Latin morphology and syntax systematically, with practice sentences and readings from Cicero, Caesar, Virgil, Horace, Ovid, and others. Accompanied by the *Wheelock's Latin Reader* for intermediate students.
