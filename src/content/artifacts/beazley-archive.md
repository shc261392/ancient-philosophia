## About the Archive

The **Beazley Archive Pottery Database (BAPD)** was founded on the work of Sir John Beazley (1885–1970), who systematically attributed thousands of Attic vases to individual painters and workshops. The database is the essential starting point for any study of Greek vase painting.

## How to Use

- Search by **painter** (e.g., Euphronios, Exekias, the Berlin Painter)
- Search by **shape** (amphora, krater, kylix, lekythos, etc.)
- Search by **subject** (mythology, athletics, daily life, etc.)
- Search by **museum** and **provenance**
- Link to images in museum collections worldwide
