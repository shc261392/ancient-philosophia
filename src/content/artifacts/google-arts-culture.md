## Highlights for Ancient History Students

Google Arts & Culture partners with major museums to provide:

- **Virtual Museum Tours** — Walk through galleries of the British Museum, Met, Louvre, and many more
- **Street View Heritage** — Navigate archaeological sites including Pompeii, the Athenian Acropolis, Luxor Temple, and Persepolis
- **High-Resolution Imagery** — Zoom into details of pottery painting, seal impressions, and inscriptions
- **Curated Exhibitions** — Thematic online exhibitions on topics like "Ancient Egypt" and "The Art of Ancient Greece"
