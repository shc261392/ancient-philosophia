## About the Collection

The British Museum holds over **8 million objects** spanning two million years of human history. Its ancient world collections are among the most important anywhere:

- **Ancient Egypt and Sudan** — Over 100,000 objects, including the Rosetta Stone, mummies, coffins, papyri, and sculpture
- **Middle East** — Major Assyrian, Babylonian, and Sumerian collections, including Layard's reliefs from Nimrud and Nineveh
- **Greece and Rome** — The Parthenon sculptures, the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, and the Nereid Monument

## Online Collection

The BM's online database provides images and descriptions for millions of objects. Many images are available under a **Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0** license.
