Herbert Weir Smyth's *Greek Grammar* (1920, revised 1956) remains the definitive reference grammar of ancient Greek in English. It covers phonology, morphology, and syntax comprehensively, with extensive treatment of dialect forms (Attic, Ionic, Doric, Aeolic, Homeric). Available free online through the Perseus Digital Library. Essential for any student beyond the beginner level.
