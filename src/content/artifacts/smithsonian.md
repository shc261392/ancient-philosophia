## About the Collection

The Smithsonian's anthropology department houses significant ancient world holdings. While less famous for antiquities than the Met or British Museum, its collections include important Egyptian, Mesopotamian, and Classical objects. The Smithsonian also leads in **3D digitization** of artifacts.

## Online Access

The Smithsonian Open Access initiative provides CC0 images and 3D models. Visit **[3D.si.edu](https://3d.si.edu/)** for rotatable 3D scans of artifacts.
