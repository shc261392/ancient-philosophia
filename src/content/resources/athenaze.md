*Athenaze* by Maurice Balme and Gilbert Lawall is one of the most widely used introductory Greek textbooks worldwide. Unlike traditional grammar-translation approaches, it teaches Greek through connected stories about an Athenian farmer and his family during the Persian Wars, supplemented by adapted readings from Herodotus, Thucydides, and other ancient authors. Available in two volumes from Oxford University Press.
