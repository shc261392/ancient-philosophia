*Greek: An Intensive Course* (2nd revised edition, 1992) by Hardy Hansen and Gerald Quinn is widely used in university programs for its systematic and thorough approach to Classical Attic Greek. The course covers all essential grammar in a single year with extensive drill exercises. It is more demanding than Athenaze but produces strong readers of original texts.
