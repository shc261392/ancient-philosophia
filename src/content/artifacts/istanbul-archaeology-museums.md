## About the Collection

The Istanbul Archaeology Museums comprise three buildings:

- **Archaeological Museum** — Greek and Roman sculpture and sarcophagi, including the Alexander Sarcophagus
- **Museum of the Ancient Orient** — Mesopotamian, Hittite, Urartian, and Babylonian material, including the Treaty of Kadesh and the Ishtar Gate tiles (smaller set; main gate is in Berlin)
- **Tiled Kiosk Museum** — Ottoman-era kiosk with Seljuk and Ottoman ceramics

## Significance for Hittitology

Istanbul holds major collections of Hittite artifacts from excavations across Anatolia, including cuneiform tablets, reliefs, and seals from Hattusa (Boğazköy) and other sites.
