*Egyptian Grammar* by Sir Alan Gardiner (3rd edition, 1957) is the classic reference grammar of Middle Egyptian. While newer textbooks (Allen, Hoch) are preferred for learning, Gardiner's sign list remains the standard classification system for hieroglyphic signs used in Egyptology worldwide. The grammar includes extensive reading exercises with hieroglyphic texts.
